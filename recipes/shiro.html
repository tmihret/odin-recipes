How to make Ethiopian Shiro Wot

Description

This Ethiopian Dish is made out of chickpeas made into powder and is served with injera. It is very delicious and easy to make if you have the shiro powder which you can buy from your local Ethiopian store. Let's get started!

Ingridients

  • Shiro Powder
  • Water
  • Olive Oil
  • Diced Onions
  • Injera

Steps

  1. Add oil and the diced onions to the pot
  2. Let the onions cook till they're light brown
  3. Fill a pot about 3/4 way with water
  4. Let the water boil a little
  5. Add the shiro powder
  6. Shut the pot with a lid, occasionally stir and wait for the shiro to boil
  7. Shut off the stove when you see bubbles forming
  8. Serve it on injera and enjoy!
