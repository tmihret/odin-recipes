How to make Japanese Ramen

Description

This recipe describes how to make an authetic Japanese ramen bowl using shoyu broth made from soy sauce topped with different ingridients like chicken, eggs and more! It is very easy to make.

Ingridients

  • Noodles
  • Soy Sauce
  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Chicken
  • Scallions
  • Chicken Broth

Steps

  1. Make the broth using the chicken broth and add soy sauce
  2. Cook the noodles in boiling water for about ten minutes till tender
  3. Boil the eggs for about seven minutes on medium heat for soft boiled eggs
  4. Cut your scalliops into pieces
  5. Put them all in a bowl by first putting on the broth then the toppings
  6. Enjoy!
