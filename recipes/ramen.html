How to make Japanese Ramen

Description

This recipe describes how to make an authetic Japanese ramen bowl using shoyu broth made from soy sauce topped with different ingridients like chicken, eggs and more! It is very easy to make.

Ingridients

  • Noodles
  • Soy Sauce
  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Chicken
  • Scallions
  • Chicken Broth
