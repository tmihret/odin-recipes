How to make a Steak

Description

In this steak tutorial, we will teach you how to cook a nice juicy steak with simple steps and no overcomplication. Let's get started!

Ingridients

  • Flat iron steak
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • BBQ rub

Steps

  1. Get the flat iron steak and season it with salt, pepper and the bbq rub, make sure to get all the sides
  2. Get a stick of butter and put it on a pan on medium-high heat
  3. When the butter is mostly melted, put the seasoned meat on the pan
  4. Add the cloves of garlic without the skin on the pan, and sear the steak with more butter for about 10-12 minutes
  5. Serve on a plate with sides and sauce and enjoy!
